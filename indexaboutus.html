THE DEN CREEK

Perfect for a quick meal or a prompt business meet, it is both a gastronomic and sensory delight. Food prepared in interactive counters on modern cooking ranges, grills and exotic clay ovens display the innovative art-form of our chefs. Walk out to Alfresco for an open air leisurely space that stands out for its unique feature of being surrounded by a trail of all day flowing water body that rejuvenates the traveller and personifies the name, The Creek.
